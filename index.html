ABOUT RIKO

Riko is the main character of the anime and manga series, Made in Abyss. In a quest to find her mother, Riko journeys to the bottom of the Abyss.

  1. She is 12 years old
  2. She is an orphan
  3. She wants to become a White Whistle cave raider
  4. Riko is a curious, cute, and smol child that must be protected at all costs

Riko's Precious People

Riko is on a quest to find clues about her mother, Lyza, one of the legendary White Whistle cave raiders of her town, Orth. Here are the people she treasures most!

LYZA

Lyza "The Annihilator" is a legendary White Whistle delver known for her fiery personality.

JIRUO

Jiruo is a teacher and caretaker at Riko's orphanage and one of Riko's greatest supporters.

REG

Reg was found by Riko during a trip to the 1st layer of the Abyss. He is a humanoid treasure with amazing firepower.

NANACHI

Nanachi is a Narehate, a human who survives ascending from the 6th layer of the Abyss. Riko and Reg meet Nanachi when they reach the 4th layer.
