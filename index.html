ABOUT RIKO

Riko is the main character of the anime and manga series, Made in Abyss. In a quest to find her mother, Riko journeys to the bottom of the Abyss.

  1. She's 12 years old
  2. She is an orphan
  3. She is the daughter of a legendary White Whistle cave raider
  4. Riko is a curious, cute, and smol child that must be protected at all costs

Riko's Precious People

Riko travels with a humanoid Abyss treasure, Reg, and an Abyss dweller called Nanachi.
